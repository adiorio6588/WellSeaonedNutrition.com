About

Hello! My name is Kimaya Joshi and thank you for checking out my site. I started this private practice for people who want to live a more nutritious lifestyle without having to abandon their well-seasoned cultural foods. Good news, you don’t have to! I pride myself on understanding food from different cultures and continuing to learn from my clients day by day. I can help you eat the foods you love, while still reaching your goals.

Background

Kimaya graduated from Saint Louis University with a bachelor of science in nutrition and dietetics as well as minors in public health and interdisciplinary education (the 1st of it’s kind in the nation). Her dietetic residency was completed at the Veterans Administration Hospital in Chicago. She then worked to promote and implement nutrition and physical activity curriculum into school districts (elementary, middle, and high schools). In Chicagoland she also co-created the Junior Green Youth Farm at the Roberti Community House with support from the Chicago Botanic Gardens. Since then, she has moved to Los Angeles and provides medical nutrition therapy for adults at a local hospital and conducts food service inspections for adult residential facilities. In addition to professional education, Kimaya has worked in the food industry for 6+ years. From boutique plant based restaurants to hospital tray lines and delivery, she is well seasoned with food in all stages of farm to table.
